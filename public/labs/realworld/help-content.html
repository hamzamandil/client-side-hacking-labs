Help Center

How do I share a document?

Click the share icon in the top-right corner of any document. You can share via email or generate a public link.

How do I set permissions?

Go to Settings > Team > Permissions to configure role-based access for your workspace.

Where are deleted files?

Deleted files are moved to Trash and kept for 30 days before permanent deletion.
